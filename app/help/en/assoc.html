Definition
n1 is the number of categories of field 1.
n2 is the number of categories of field 2.

Display various quantities computed from the contingency table of the two fields :
  • The phi coefficient (if n1=2 and n2=2)
  • The tetrachoric correlation (approximed)
  • The C coefficient
  • The Cramér's V coefficient
  • The mutual mutual information
  • The uncertainty coefficient
  • The Goodman and Kruskal's lambda coefficient
Parameters
In the top slot, the first field.
In the left slot, the second field.
