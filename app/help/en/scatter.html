Definition
Representation of data points in a cartesian coordinate system.
The green line represents the regression line of the two variables.
Parameters
In the top slot, the quantitative field to be represented along axis X.
In the left slot, the quantitative field to be represented along axis Y.
In the bottom slot, an optional qualitative field used to color the points .
In the right slot, an optional qualitative field used to label the points.
Actions
Drag a numerical field to the graph to change its unit. The points will be represented as bubbles of size proportional to the numerical value.
