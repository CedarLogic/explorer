Definition
Representation of the data points in a 3 dimension cartesian system.

Parameters
In the right slots, the numerical fields used as coordinates.
The first field is displayed along the X axis (from left to right)
The second field is display along the Y axis (from bottom to top)
The third field is display along the Z axis (toward the observer)
Other fields are ignored.

In the left slot, an option categorical field used to color the points.

Actions
Drag the special numerical field "1" to add all the fields.
