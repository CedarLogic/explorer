Definition
Two categories a and b of the field 1 are "associated" if there exist observations (x,a) and (x,b) with the same category x of the field 2.
Parameters
In the top slot, the categorical field 1 whose categories are associated.
In the left slot, the categorical field 2 allowing the associations.
Options
Option 1: the categories of the field 1 are displayed along a line, in alphabetic order. The height of the arc between two categories is proportional to the number of associations by the field 2.

Option 2: the categories of the field 1 are displayed along a circle.
