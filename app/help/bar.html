Definition
Vertical bars with height proportional to the value they represent.
The categories are displayed from left to right by alphabetic order.
Parameters
In the top slot, the quantitative field whose categories are to be represented.
In the left slot, the quantitative field whose breakdown is to be represented.
Options
Option 1: Normal chart. The height of the bar is proportional to the weight of the corresponding category.

Option 2: Percentage chart. Within each bar, the colors represent the breakdown of the second field in the corresponding category.
