Definition
Representation of data points in a cartesian coordinate system.
The green line represents the regression line of the two variables.
Parameters
In the top slot, the quantitative field to be represented along axis X.
In the left slot, the quantitative field to be represented along axis Y.
In the bottom slot, an option qualitative field to color the points .
Actions
Drag a qualitative field to the graph to display the name of the category instead of a point.
Drag the same field again to remove the texts.
